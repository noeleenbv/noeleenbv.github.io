About Me

Who I am, what I do.

Hello and welcome to my portfolio site. This site showcases a selection of projects I’ve worked on throughout my career, along with some personal creative endeavours.

With over seven years of professional experience across various industries, I’ve had the opportunity to grow in roles such as an Artworker, Print Specialist, Web Designer/Developer, and even Online Ecommerce Business Owner.

The last 8 years I took a break from my career and focused on being a dedicated mum to my two children. Now, I’m excited to dive back into my professional passions with renewed energy and a fresh perspective.

I’m particularly inspired by the rapid advancements in AI and its potential to transform industries. I look forward to embracing these innovations and integrating them into my work as I continue to grow and evolve as a creative professional.

If you're interested in collaborating or have any questions, I'd love to hear from you. Please feel free to reach out via email or mobile — I'm excited about the opportunity to work together!

Lovefetti

Product packaging & marketing design

A biodegradable wedding confetti product that I created for my company and sold on Amazon.

I created the Lovefetti brand, designed the artwork, packaging labels, and infographics for its Amazon listing, while also capturing and optimizing product photos using a Canon EOS R50 and a lightbox for Amazon and social media.

Additionally, I generated a realistic wedding couple image (using Lexica.art) and edited this in Photoshop to incorporate real flower petals.

  • Project Date: 2022
  • Client: My own business - Sissono Ltd

Daylesford

Microsite shop design & development

A responsive microsite shop created for Daylesford Organic Farm, Gloucestershire. I was involved in the project from the concept stage through to completion, responsible for the design, coding, and testing. I worked alongside a project manager who liaised directly with the client to acquire assets, gather feedback, and obtain approval.

The shop was designed in Fireworks, coded in Dreamweaver, and then uploaded onto the Ocado website using an in-house content management system. Pages were tested on hidden aisle links, signed off internally, and then submitted to the client for approval. The shop featured HD video content in dropdown banners across various pages, including the "Home" and "Our Dairy" pages.

This shop design version is no longer live, but here is the live aisle link for reference: https://www.ocado.com/webshop/getBrandShop.go?shopId=12341

  • Project Date: 2015
  • Client: Ocado / Daylesford

Pampers

Email design & coding

A baby incentive email that was designed to encourage customers to shop. Customers received five free packs of Pampers nappies with their order when they applied a voucher code at checkout.

The email was based on a standard template and aligned with the look and feel of a baby event live on the Ocado website at the time. I was responsible for the design, coding, and testing.

Designed in Fireworks, coded in Dreamweaver, and tested using Litmus and an in-house platform.

  • Project Date: 2015
  • Client: Ocado / Pampers

Ocado

Christmas microsite shop design

A responsive microsite shop designed for the Ocado Christmas event in 2013. This large-scale project spanned approximately sixty pages and involved close collaboration with teams across art direction, design, copywriting, photography, and development.

My role included cutting out product shots, page design, layout, and final checks before launch.

The first two screenshots showcase the final shop design, featuring the "Christmas Dinner" and "Decorations & Crackers" pages.

The third screenshot presents an alternative landing page concept I created, incorporating custom-designed icons to represent each category. Although this concept was not used, as product photography was preferred, it demonstrated a different visual approach.

  • Client: Ocado
