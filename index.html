About Me

Who I am, what I do.

  • I am originally from South Africa and have been living and working in the UK for the past ten and a half years now.

    As a designer at heart and a developer in mind, I enjoy expressing creativity through visual design as well as technical implementation.

Tribal Tiger

I am a fan of big cats — especially tigers. For this work I sketched the right half, then took it into Photoshop for mirroring. I was curious to see what it would look like as a vector, so I took into illustrator and traced lines over the top.

Created using graphite pencils, Photoshop and Illustrator.

  • Client: Personal
  • Category: Illustration
